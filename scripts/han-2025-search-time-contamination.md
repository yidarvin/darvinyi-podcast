[O] Nora, what should actually change because of this?
[G] The paper is fairly direct here. The old defenses don't transfer. Canary strings only work if every redistributor includes them, and the authors found third parties stripping them out, including cases where someone sliced the canary string specifically out of HLE and GPQA uploads before re-posting. Gating on HuggingFace doesn't help either, any user with legitimate access can fork and re-upload publicly, intentionally or not.
