[G] Exactly, and the report has a nice historical framing for this in figure one. BERT, or something descended from it, is now sitting underneath an enormous fraction of NLP research, and the report calls that interaction between emergence and homogenization, quote, potentially unsettling.

[G] Precisely. And the mechanism they highlight is inheritance. If the base model has a bias, a blind spot, a security hole, every single application adapted from it inherits that same defect.
