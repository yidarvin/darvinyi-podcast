[G] It's a shortcut, a feature in the training data that correlates with the correct answer without actually causing it. [G] Supervised fine-tuning, SFT, just maximizes the likelihood of the chosen response. [G] Precisely, and that gap is exactly where you'd expect the shortcut behavior to diverge.
