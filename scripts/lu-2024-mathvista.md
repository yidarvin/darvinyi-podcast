[G] Tight. [S] Now, these models generate long, chatty paragraphs, not a clean multiple-choice letter. How do you grade that deterministically?
[G] That's actually the quietly important contribution here. [O] That's clever, because it means you get deterministic grading without forcing the model into a rigid answer format that might itself hurt performance.
